The goal of Emergency Social Network is to provide civilians with a social network that they can use during emergency situations. After you logged in the app, you may set up your current status in “me” page at any time.

OK
We provide these four different status: OK means I am OK, I do not need help. We use Green to label this status; Help means I need help, but this is not a life threatening emergency. We use Yellow to label this status; Emergency means I need help now: this is a life threatening emergency! We use Red to label this status. If you do not set any status or you do not want to provide your status, you can set your status as No Status. We use gray to label this status.
